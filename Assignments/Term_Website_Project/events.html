Cycle the Continental Divide

Annual Fun Ride

April 24th 2018

Route:

  • Start in Bozeman "Big Sky Bank" parking lot go up Main Street till it turns into Old Farm Route 34. - - - 5.2 miles
  • Head North on Old Farm route 34 - - - 35.5 miles / 56 ft elevation
  • Turn Right on to Mt Pass and go over dead horse saddle.- - - 45 miles / 1,230 ft elevation
  • Turn right on Gilder's Flower Lane.- - - 24.3 miles / 154 ft elevation
  • Turn left on to North Main Street back to "Big Sky Bank". - - - 1.2 miles / 25 ft elevation

$75 per rider/$85 per tandem.

Requirements:

  • Must be able to ride over 130 miles in a day.
  • Bring water and snacks. (breakfast and luch will be provided)
  • Bike must be check with Moutain Spoke Cyclery prior to ride to make sure able to make the trek.
  • All riders must wear helmet ●ASTM's F1447 certified ● (No rider will be permitted to ride without certified helmet!).
